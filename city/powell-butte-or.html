Team Roping Events in Powell Butte, Oregon

Powell Butte, Oregon is the heart of the Central Oregon team roping community, drawing competitors from Bend, Redmond, Prineville, and the surrounding high desert region. The rural ranching character of Crook County and the broader Central Oregon plateau creates a natural environment for competitive roping culture, with arena facilities in and around Powell Butte hosting regular jackpots that draw strong regional participation. Oregon's team roping calendar is concentrated primarily in spring through fall, with the high desert climate supporting outdoor events from April through October. Powell Butte events draw ropers from across Oregon and regularly pull competitors from neighboring Washington, Idaho, and Northern California. Entry fees typically run $100 to $200 per man, with events organized across multiple USTRC number divisions. The competitive level in Central Oregon has risen significantly over the past decade as the region's roping community has grown and the quality of local rope horses has improved.
