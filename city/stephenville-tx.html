Team Roping Events in Stephenville, Texas

Stephenville, Texas earns its reputation as the Cowboy Capital of the World through a combination of deep ranching roots and a team roping culture that runs from backyard jackpots to professional-level competition. Erath County supports more active ropers per capita than almost any county in the United States, and Stephenville is the center of that gravity. Events in Stephenville run year-round across multiple arena facilities, with jackpots organized across all USTRC number divisions from the #10 through the open. The competitive level is consistently high — Stephenville has produced multiple NFR qualifiers and hosts events where professional-level ropers mix with serious amateurs in the same draw. Entry fees range from $100 to $400 per man depending on event format, with major ropings offering substantial added money and cattle counts supporting four or more runs per entry. The Fort Worth metro is an hour east, giving Stephenville access to one of the largest pools of team roping competitors in the country. Jackpot frequency is high — multiple events run on any given weekend — making it one of the best markets in the country for a roper looking to accumulate runs and competitive experience at every level.
