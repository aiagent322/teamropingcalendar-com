Team Roping Events in Stanton, Texas

Stanton, Texas is a competitive team roping community in Martin County at the heart of West Texas ranch country. The Midland-Odessa Permian Basin corridor fuels both the local economy and the roping culture, with arena facilities in and around Stanton hosting regular jackpots that draw from one of the wealthiest and most roping-active markets in West Texas. Permian Basin ropers are known for their enthusiasm and their willingness to invest in quality horses and equipment, creating a competitive environment that supports active jackpot circuits despite the region's relatively low population density. Events in Stanton draw competitors from Midland, Odessa, Big Spring, and the surrounding ranch communities of Martin, Howard, and Dawson Counties. Entry fees typically run $100 to $300 per man. The region's strong Corriente cattle supply through West Texas ranching operations supports quality event production.
