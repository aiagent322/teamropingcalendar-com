Team Roping Events in Maypearl, Texas

Maypearl, Texas is a competitive team roping hub in Ellis County, drawing competitors from the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex and the broader North Texas roping circuit. The area's agricultural character and proximity to major population centers create a strong foundation for consistent roping activity, with arena facilities in and around Maypearl hosting regular jackpots across all USTRC number divisions. Ellis County and the Maypearl corridor sit within easy driving distance of Fort Worth, Waxahachie, and Hillsboro, giving events here access to one of the largest pools of team roping competitors in the country. Entry fees run $100 to $350 per man depending on event format and size, with competitive fields that include both serious amateurs and ropers with professional circuit experience. The region's strong Quarter Horse breeding and training industry means quality rope horses are readily available, and the competitive depth at Maypearl events reflects the overall strength of the North Texas roping market.
