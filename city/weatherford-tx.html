Team Roping Events in Weatherford, Texas

Weatherford, Texas anchors the western end of the Fort Worth roping corridor and operates one of the most consistent team roping calendars in Parker County. Multiple arena facilities within and around Weatherford host weekly jackpots year-round, drawing competitors from Fort Worth, Granbury, Mineral Wells, and the surrounding Metroplex communities. The competitive level at Weatherford events spans the full range — beginners in the #10 draw share weekends with experienced competitors who rope in the #15 and open divisions. Entry fees run $100 to $350 per man depending on event size and format, with most jackpots offering 75 to 80 percent payback. Weatherford's location on Interstate 20 makes it highly accessible from the Dallas-Fort Worth metro, contributing to large and competitive fields at major events. The region's rich Quarter Horse and rope horse industry supports a strong local market for competitive heading and heeling horses, and many of the area's top trainers and rope horse producers are based within a short drive of Weatherford's active arenas.
