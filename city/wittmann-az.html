Team Roping Events in Wittmann, Arizona

Wittmann, Arizona is one of the most consistently active team roping markets in the Phoenix metro area, anchored by 4D Arena — one of the state's premier roping facilities. Located on Patton Road in the West Valley, 4D Arena operates one of the most aggressive roping schedules in Arizona, with Tuesday and Thursday slide events, Saturday breakaway ropings, and periodic major jackpots drawing competitors from across the Phoenix metro and beyond. The arena's professional management, quality cattle, and consistent schedule have made it a destination for serious competitors in the #10 through open divisions. 4D Arena requires a Global Handicap Card and photo ID, and runs cash-preferred operations with multiple entry options per event. Entry fees run $120 to $200 per man for standard jackpots, with cattle counts supporting four entries or more. The arena sits in the northwest Phoenix corridor, accessible from the West Valley, Wickenburg, and the Prescott area.
