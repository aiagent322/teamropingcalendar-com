Team Roping Events in Buckeye, Arizona

Buckeye, Arizona is one of the fastest-growing team roping markets in the West Valley, benefiting from the rapid expansion of the far west Phoenix metro area and a strong equestrian community that has grown alongside the region's development. Arena facilities in and around Buckeye host regular jackpots drawing competitors from the West Valley, Goodyear, Avondale, and the outlying desert communities to the west. The competitive calendar runs strongest from October through April, taking advantage of the desert climate before summer heat peaks. Buckeye events span all USTRC number divisions, with both beginner-friendly #10 jackpots and more competitive open draws running on the same weekends at area facilities. Entry fees run $100 to $250 per man. The region's strong Quarter Horse and rope horse community means Buckeye ropers have good access to competitive horses, and several area trainers specialize in developing heading and heeling horses for the local roping market. TeamRopingCalendar.com tracks team roping events in Buckeye and the broader West Valley roping corridor.
