Team Roping Events in Llano, Texas

Llano, Texas sits at the geographic and cultural center of the Texas Hill Country roping community, drawing competitors from San Antonio, Austin, Fredericksburg, and the surrounding ranch country that defines this part of the state. The Hill Country's strong cattle ranching tradition translates directly into a vibrant team roping culture, with Llano area arenas hosting regular jackpots across all USTRC number divisions. Events in Llano range from small local ropings serving the immediate community to larger regional jackpots that bring in competitors from across central and south Texas. Entry fees run $100 to $300 per man depending on event size, with quality Corriente cattle sourced from Hill Country ranching operations. The competitive level reflects the area's strong ranching roots — Llano ropers grow up around cattle and horses, creating a technically sound competitor base at all number levels. Llano's location on Highway 16 makes it accessible from the San Antonio corridor in roughly two hours and from Austin in under ninety minutes.
