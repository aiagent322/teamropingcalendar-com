Team Roping Events in Homedale, Idaho

Homedale, Idaho is the team roping center of the Treasure Valley, drawing competitors from Nampa, Caldwell, Boise, and the surrounding southwestern Idaho agricultural region. Owyhee County's ranching heritage and the Snake River Plain's strong agricultural economy create a natural foundation for competitive roping culture. Arena facilities in and around Homedale host regular jackpots drawing competitors from throughout southwestern Idaho and eastern Oregon, with events organized across multiple USTRC number divisions. Idaho's team roping calendar concentrates primarily in spring through fall, with the strongest activity running from April through October. The competitive level in the Treasure Valley has risen significantly as the region's population and roping community have grown. Entry fees typically run $100 to $200 per man.
