Team Roping Events in Amarillo, Texas

Amarillo, Texas is the team roping hub of the Texas Panhandle, anchoring one of the most geographically expansive roping markets in the country. The Panhandle's flat, wide-open ranch country and feedlot-driven cattle economy create ideal conditions for team roping culture, with multiple arena facilities in and around Amarillo hosting regular jackpots year-round. Events here draw competitors from across the Panhandle, as well as neighboring New Mexico, Oklahoma, and Colorado, giving Amarillo ropings one of the largest geographic footprints of any regional market in the sport. Entry fees run $100 to $350 per man depending on event format, with quality Corriente cattle available through the region's massive cattle industry infrastructure. The competitive level spans all USTRC number divisions, with serious open competitors mixing with a strong amateur base.
