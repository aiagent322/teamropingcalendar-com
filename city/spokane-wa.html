Team Roping Events in Spokane, Washington

Spokane, Washington is the largest team roping market in the Pacific Northwest, anchoring the eastern Washington roping circuit and drawing competitors from Idaho, Montana, and the broader Inland Northwest region. Multiple arena facilities in the Spokane Valley and surrounding area host regular jackpots year-round, with the strongest calendar running from April through October. Spokane events attract a mix of serious regional competitors and dedicated amateurs across all USTRC number classifications, with some events drawing entry lists that rival the quality of mid-size Texas jackpots. The proximity to northern Idaho — particularly the Coeur d'Alene and Moscow corridors — gives Spokane events access to a broader competitive pool than the city's metro size alone would suggest. Entry fees typically run $100 to $250 per man. Spokane's strong rodeo tradition, anchored by the Spokane Interstate Fair rodeo, creates a deep bench of competitive western athletes who transition into jackpot roping.
