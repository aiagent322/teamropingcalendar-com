Team Roping Events in Wickenburg, Arizona

Wickenburg, Arizona is one of the most active team roping communities in the American Southwest, drawing headers and heelers from across the greater Phoenix metro area, Prescott, and the outlying desert corridor year-round. The town's strong ranching heritage and wide-open desert geography have made it a natural hub for roping culture, with multiple arena facilities hosting weekly and monthly events across all USTRC number classifications. Rancho Rio is the anchor facility for Wickenburg roping, hosting everything from Monday night jackpots to major finals events with significant added money. Entry fees at Wickenburg events typically range from $120 to $300 per man depending on the size and format of the roping, with most events offering 70 to 80 percent payback. The NTR National Finals and Kevin Redstrom events bring in competitors from across Arizona and neighboring states, elevating the competitive level well above a typical local jackpot. Wickenburg's location on Highway 60 makes it easily accessible from Phoenix in under an hour, and the mild winter climate supports a packed fall through spring calendar. Whether you rope in the #10 draw or compete in the open, Wickenburg consistently delivers quality cattle, competitive fields, and well-run events.
