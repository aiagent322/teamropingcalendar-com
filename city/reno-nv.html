Team Roping Events in Reno, Nevada

Reno, Nevada is the team roping capital of the Pacific region and one of the most strategically important roping markets in the country, particularly because of its proximity to Las Vegas and the annual convergence of roping talent during NFR week in December. Throughout the year, Reno supports a strong local jackpot circuit drawing competitors from Northern California, Oregon, Idaho, and the broader Mountain West. Arena facilities in and around Reno host regular jackpots across multiple number divisions, with the Reno-Sparks area offering events ranging from small weekly draws to regional qualifiers for major series. The World Series of Team Roping draws thousands of competitors through the Las Vegas corridor annually, and Reno serves as a qualifying hub with events feeding that pipeline. Entry fees at Reno events typically run $150 to $300 per man depending on format. The high desert climate supports a strong spring and fall calendar, with summer events also active. Reno ropers benefit from quality cattle supply from Nevada and California ranching operations, and the competitive pool includes some of the strongest west coast talent outside of Texas.
